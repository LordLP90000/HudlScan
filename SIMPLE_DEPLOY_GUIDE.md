## 🌐 Better Method: Get Your Own Website URL

Want to share with your team? Get a permanent website link everyone can use.

### Option B: Deploy to Netlify (Easiest Sharing - No Tech Skills)

**Time Required**: 5 minutes  
**Cost**: Free forever  
**Result**: Get a URL like `yourteam-playbook.netlify.app`

#### Step-by-Step:

1. **Download the Files**
   - Click the download button on each file in the outputs
   - Save them all to a folder on your computer
   - Name the folder something like "playbook-converter"

2. **Go to Netlify**
   - Visit: https://www.netlify.com
   - Click "Sign Up" (use your email or GitHub)
   - Completely free, no credit card needed

3. **Deploy Your Site**
   - Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
   - **Drag and drop your entire folder** onto the box
   - Wait 30 seconds while it uploads
   - **Done!** Netlify gives you a link

4. **Customize Your URL** (optional)
   - Click "Site settings"
   - Click "Change site name"
   - Pick something like: `eagles-playbook` or `coach-smith-plays`
   - Now your URL is: `eagles-playbook.netlify.app`

5. **Share with Team**
   - Send the URL to players/coaches
   - Everyone can use it from their phone/computer
   - No installation needed!

**Video Tutorial**: Search YouTube for "How to deploy to Netlify drag and drop"

---

### Option C: Deploy to Vercel (Also Free & Easy)

**Time Required**: 10 minutes (includes one-time setup)  
**Cost**: Free forever  
**Result**: Get a URL like `yourteam-playbook.vercel.app`

#### Step-by-Step:

1. **Download the Files**
   - Same as Option B above

2. **Install Node.js** (one-time setup)
   - Go to: https://nodejs.org
   - Click the big "Download" button (LTS version)
   - Run the installer (click Next, Next, Install)
   - This lets you run commands in terminal

3. **Open Terminal/Command Prompt**
   - **Windows**: Press Windows key, type "cmd", press Enter
   - **Mac**: Press Cmd+Space, type "terminal", press Enter

4. **Navigate to Your Folder**
   ```
   cd path/to/your/playbook-converter
   ```
   - **Tip for Windows**: Type `cd ` then drag your folder into the terminal window
   - **Tip for Mac**: Same thing!

5. **Install Vercel** (one-time setup)
   ```
   npm install -g vercel
   ```
   - Wait a minute while it installs

6. **Deploy**
   ```
   vercel
   ```
   - First time: It asks you to log in
   - Follow the prompts (press Enter for defaults)
   - Wait 1-2 minutes
   - **You get a URL!** Something like `playbook-abc123.vercel.app`

7. **Optional: Get Custom URL**
   - Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
   - Find your project
   - Click "Settings" → "Domains"
   - Add a custom domain or change the Vercel subdomain

**Video Tutorial**: Search YouTube for "How to deploy to Vercel for beginners"
